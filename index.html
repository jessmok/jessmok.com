WelBi

Short for "well-being," WelBi is an app that a few friends and I worked on during Camp PolyHacks, a 24-hour-long hackathon with a focus on community, education, and the environment. Our aim was to make healthy living easy, personalized, and accessible by creating a platform that crowd-sourced data and allowed users to share recipes. We wanted to take into account the importance of individualization by allowing users to sort through recipes based on body type, dietary restrictions, weight goals, etc.
